A Tribute Page

To the company that created the so called "Souls Genre" and all their recent achievements.

Here is a timeline of the greatest recent releases and achievements of FromSoftware

  • 2009 - Demon's Souls
  • 2011 - Dark Souls
  • 2014 - Dark Souls II (Game of the Year)
  • 2015 - Bloodborne (Game of the Year)
  • 2016 - Dark Souls III (Game of the Year)
  • 2018 - Dark Souls Remastered
  • 2019 - Sekiro Shadows Die Twice (Game of the Year)
  • 2020 Demon's Souls Remake (Remade by Bluepoint Games)
  • 2022 Elden Ring (Current "Game of the Year" contender)
